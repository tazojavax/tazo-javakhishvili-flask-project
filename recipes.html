🍕 Food Recipes

Pizza

Ingredients: Dough, cheese, tomato sauce.

Recipe: Spread tomato sauce on the dough, add cheese and bake at 220°C for 15 minutes.

Shawarma

Ingredients: Chicken, vegetables, lavash.

Recipe: Cook the chicken, add vegetables and wrap everything in lavash.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients: Lettuce, chicken, croutons.

Recipe: Mix lettuce, grilled chicken and croutons, then add Caesar dressing.

Khachapuri

Ingredients: Dough, cheese, egg.

Recipe: Fill dough with cheese, bake until golden and add an egg on top.

Burger

Ingredients: Beef patty, bun, cheese, lettuce, tomato.

Recipe: Grill the patty and place it inside the bun with vegetables and cheese.

Pasta

Ingredients: Pasta, tomato sauce, garlic, parmesan cheese.

Recipe: Boil pasta, add sauce and garlic, then sprinkle parmesan cheese on top.
